'''Cyril''' (also Cyrillus or Cyryl) is a masculine given name. It is derived from the [[Greek language|Greek]] name Κύριλλος (''Kyrillos'') meaning "Lordly, Masterful" which in turn derives from Greek κυριος (Kyrios) "[[Lord]]". There are various variant forms of the Cyril name such as Cyrill, Cyrille, Kirill, Kiryl, Kirillos, Kyrylo, Kiril, Kiro and Kyrill.
