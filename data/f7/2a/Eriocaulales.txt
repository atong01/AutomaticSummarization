'''''Eriocaulales''''' is a [[botanical name]] for an [[order (biology)|order]] of [[flowering plant]]s. The name was published by Nakai. In the [[Cronquist system]] the name was used for an order placed in the subclass ''[[Commelinidae]]''. The order consisted of one family only (1981):

* order ''Eriocaulales''
*: family ''[[Eriocaulaceae]]''

The [[APG II system]] assigns these plants to the order ''[[Poales]]''.
