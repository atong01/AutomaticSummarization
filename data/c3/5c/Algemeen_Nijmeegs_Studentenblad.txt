The '''''Algemeen Nijmeegs Studentenblad''''' is an independent [[Student newspaper|student magazine]] for the [[Radboud University Nijmegen]]. Founded in 1985 by members of the local [[Students' union|student union]] [[AKKU]], it is now published by the [[Stichting Multimedia]].
