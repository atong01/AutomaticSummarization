European Classical music has long relied on [[music competitions]] to provide a public forum that identifies the strongest players and contributes to the establishment of their professional careers. This is a list of current [[competition]]s in [[European classical music|classical music]]. Competitions offering a range of categories are listed under "General/mixed". Competitions with age restrictions are listed under "Young musicians".
