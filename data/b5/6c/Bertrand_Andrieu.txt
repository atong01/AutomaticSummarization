'''Bertrand Andrieu''' (24 November 1761&nbsp;&ndash; 6 December 1822) was a [[French people|French]] [[engraving|engraver]] of medals.<ref>{{Cite EB9|wstitle=Bertrand Andrieu}}</ref> He was born in [[Bordeaux]]. In [[Kingdom of France|France]], he was considered as the restorer of the art, which had declined after the time of [[Louis XIV of France|Louis XIV]]. During the last twenty years of his life, the French government commissioned him to undertake every major work of importance.<ref>{{Cite EB1911|wstitle=Bertrand Andrieu|volume=1}}</ref>
