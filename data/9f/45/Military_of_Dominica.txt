There is no [[standing army]] in [[Dominica]] since 1981.<ref>''[http://www.demilitarisation.org/spip.php?article51&lang=en Overview. 26 countries without armies]'', ARPED</ref>  Defense is the responsibility of [[Regional Security System]] (RSS).

The civil Commonwealth of Dominica Police Force includes a Special Service Unit, Coast Guard. In the event of war or other emergency, if proclaimed by the authorities, the Police Force shall be a military force which may be employed for State defence (''Police Act'', Chapter 14:01).<ref>cited literally from ''[http://www.resdal.org/atlas/atlas10-ing-09-the-caribbean.pdf The Caribbean: Defence and Security in the Anglophone Caribbean — The Roads to Cooperation].'' in ''A Comparative Atlas of Defence in Latin America and Caribbean'', 2010 Edition, table ''Defence Force’s Mission'', p. 113 (pp. 113-128, pdf p. 1)</ref>
