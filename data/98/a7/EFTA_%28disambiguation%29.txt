{{Wiktionary|EFTA}}
'''[[EFTA]]''' is the European Free Trade Association, a European trade bloc which was established as an alternative for European states who did not join the then-European Economic Community.

'''EFTA''' may also refer to:

* [[European Fair Trade Association]], an association of eleven fair trade importers in nine European countries
* [[European Free Trade Area]], other European trade blocs
* [[European Federation of Taiwanese Associations]], which combines several associations by Taiwanese people in each European country
