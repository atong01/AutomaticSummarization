The '''Government of the Republic of Belarus''' ({{lang-be|Урад Рэспублікі Беларусь}}), which consists of the '''Council of Ministers of the Republic of Belarus''' ({{Lang-be|Савет Міністраў Рэспублікі Беларусь}}), is the executive branch of state power in [[Belarus]], and it is appointed by the [[President of Belarus]]. The head of the Government is the [[Prime Minister of Belarus]], who manages the main agenda of the Government and direct the ministers.
