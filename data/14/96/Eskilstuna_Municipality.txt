'''Eskilstuna Municipality''' (''Eskilstuna kommun'') is a [[municipalities of Sweden|municipality]] in [[Södermanland County]] in southeast [[Sweden]], between the [[lake]]s [[Mälaren]] and [[Hjälmaren]]. Its seat is located in the [[city status in Sweden|city]] of [[Eskilstuna]].

The present municipality was formed in 1971 when the ''City of Eskilstuna'', the ''City of [[Torshälla]]'' and five rural municipalities were amalgamated.
