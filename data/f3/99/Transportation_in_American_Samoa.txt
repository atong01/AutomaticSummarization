American Samoa has a total of 241&nbsp;km of [[highway]]s (estimated in 2008).<ref name="CIA">[https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/geos/aq.html "American Samoa."] [https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook CIA - The World Factbook]. Accessed June 2011.</ref> Ports and [[harbor]]s include [[Aunuu|Aunu‘u]], [[Auasi]], [[Tau, Samoa|Faleāsao]], [[Ofu-Olosega#Ofu|Ofu]] and [[Pago Pago]].<ref name="CIA"/> American Samoa has no railways.<ref name="CIA"/> The territory has three [[airport]]s, all of which have paved runways. The main airport is [[Pago Pago International Airport]].<ref name="CIA"/> According to a 1999 estimate, the territory has no [[merchant marine]].<ref name="CIA"/>
