'''Europe of Democracies and Diversities''' (EDD) was a [[Euroscepticism|Eurosceptic]]<ref name="PhinnemoreMcGowan">{{cite book|author1=David Phinnemore|author2=Lee McGowan|title=A Dictionary of the European Union|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=m9EB7e8pgywC&pg=PA276|accessdate=7 August 2013|date=26 June 2013|publisher=Routledge|isbn=978-1-135-08127-0|page=276}}</ref><ref name="Trechsel2013">{{cite book|author=Alexander H. Trechsel|title=Towards a Federal Europe|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=mTndAAAAQBAJ&pg=PT72|date=13 September 2013|publisher=Taylor & Francis|isbn=978-1-317-99818-1|page=72}}</ref> [[Political groups of the European Parliament|political group]] with seats in the [[European Parliament]] between 1999 and 2004. Following the [[European Parliament election, 2004|2004 European elections]], the group reformed as [[Independence/Democracy]] (IND/DEM).<ref name="PhinnemoreMcGowan"/>
